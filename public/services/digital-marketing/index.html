The Problem

Most marketing spend is invisible. Campaigns run without clear attribution. SEO produces rankings for keywords that don't convert. Social media content generates engagement but zero pipeline.

Agencies optimise for vanity metrics — impressions, followers, reach — because those are easy to report. Revenue-generating activity is harder to track and requires deeper integration with sales data.

AximoIX treats marketing as an engineering problem: instrument the funnel, measure what matters, and iterate fast.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does AximoIX handle both B2B and B2C marketing?

Yes. AximoIX has delivered marketing programmes for B2B technology companies, B2C e-commerce brands, financial services firms, and government organisations. Channel mix and KPI frameworks are adjusted for each sales cycle length and conversion model.

Does AximoIX optimise for AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity)?

Yes. AximoIX specialises in Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) and Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) — restructuring page content and schema markup to appear in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.

What reporting does AximoIX provide?

AximoIX provides monthly performance reports covering agreed KPIs with attribution modelling. Dashboards are built in Google Looker Studio or Tableau and shared with client stakeholders. Quarterly strategy reviews assess channel mix and budget allocation.
