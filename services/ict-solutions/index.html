The Problem

Legacy on-premises infrastructure creates three compounding problems: unpredictable downtime, escalating maintenance costs, and inability to scale with demand.

Most organisations manage IT reactively — responding to outages rather than preventing them. Patching cycles are slow. Security posture is inconsistent. Cloud costs spiral without governance.

The result: IT becomes a brake on business velocity rather than an accelerator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does AximoIX manage on-premises or cloud-only infrastructure?

Both. AximoIX manages on-premises, cloud-native, and hybrid environments. Many client engagements begin with on-premises assessment and transition to a hybrid or cloud-first model over 6-18 months.

How is the 99.9% uptime SLA enforced?

The SLA is backed by 24/7 monitoring, documented incident response procedures, automated alerting, and contractual service credits for downtime exceeding agreed thresholds. RTO and RPO targets are agreed at contract stage and tested during quarterly DR exercises.

Which cloud platforms does AximoIX support?

AximoIX architects and manages workloads on Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). Multi-cloud and hybrid-cloud architectures are also supported, including unified cost governance across providers.

How long does a cloud migration take?

Migration timelines depend on workload complexity and size. A single-application migration can complete in 2-4 weeks. A full enterprise migration (500+ users, mixed workloads) typically takes 3-9 months in phases. AximoIX delivers in 2-week sprints with continuous stakeholder reporting throughout.
